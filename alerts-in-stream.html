How the Sponsorship Works

The creator promotes the game during their live stream while encouraging viewers to participate in the campaign.

A sponsorship widget is displayed on stream, tracking campaign progress in real time and showing the goals viewers need to complete.

As viewers reach milestones—such as installs, sign-ups, or other campaign objectives—the widget updates automatically. Each completed goal triggers an on-stream alert, highlighting the viewer who achieved it and creating engaging moments for the entire community.

The more goals the audience completes, the more campaign progress the creator unlocks, turning viewer participation into rewards and measurable results.
