About me

I'm Pilar Meaca, a Graphic and Motion Designer,
based in Buenos Aires, Argentina,.

For more than 15 years, I've been creating motion graphics,
branding, and visual experiences across broadcast
and digital products.
My background includes work for TV production studios,
design agencies, Fox Latin America Channels, and
The Walt Disney Company.

Most recently, I spent six years at StreamElements, designing
creator-focused products, sponsorship experiences, and interactive tools for live streaming platforms such as Twitch and YouTube.
