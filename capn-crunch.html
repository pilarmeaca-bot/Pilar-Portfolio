art direction / design

Client: StreamElements

How the Sponsorship Works

The creator promotes the brand during their live stream and encourages viewers to complete the campaign action.

A sponsorship widget is displayed on stream, tracking the campaign's progress in real time and showing how many actions are needed to reach each milestone.

As viewers participate, the widget updates automatically. Every completed action contributes to the campaign goal and triggers an on-stream alert, highlighting the viewer and creating engaging moments for the entire community.

The more the audience completes, the further the campaign progresses, helping the creator unlock rewards while driving measurable results for the brand.

A branded full-screen animated takeover that temporarily takes over
